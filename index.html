About me

My name is Velian /ˈvɛliən/ and I have an M.Sc. in Human-Computer Interaction from the University of Toronto.

I am a UX professional, a trained qualitative researcher, and a seasoned lecturer in HCI and computer science.

I believe that solving real-world problems requires context-aware, rich insights into users' underlying goals and motivations.

I can deliver these insights and turn them into potential solutions through research, ideation, prototyping, and evaluation.
